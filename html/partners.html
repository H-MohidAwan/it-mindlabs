PARTNERS

Our partners are the top companies in the industry.

Our clients rely on us to resolve their intricate IAM (Identity and Access Management) challenges, and they require the top-notch solutions. That's why we collaborate with both emerging startups and established leaders in the industry to provide services and solutions that are cutting-edge, agile, and trustworthy.

Integral Partners is not biased towards any particular vendor. This means that we use our extensive knowledge of products to help you select solutions that are most suitable for your organization. We collaborate and partner with top Identity and Access Management (IAM) companies to ensure that you receive the best possible assistance for your implementations, integrations, and customizations.

SailPoint helps global organizations to securely and effectively manage user access from any device to data and applications that are residing in the data center, on mobile devices, and in the cloud. The company offers an innovative product portfolio that provides customers with an integrated set of core services, including identity governance, provisioning, and access management. These services can be delivered on-premises or from the cloud (IAM-as-a-service). With AI and machine learning at its core, the SailPoint identity security cloud platform helps you to see and understand all your identities and their access at speed and scale. The insights provided by the platform help you stay ahead of potential security threats.

Saviynt is a reliable cloud-based Access Governance solution provider that partners with leading infrastructure, applications, and framework technology providers. They specialize in providing innovative application, data, and infrastructure access governance and intelligence platforms for both Cloud and Enterprise. Saviynt delivers IGA 2.0 by integrating advanced usage and risk analytics with fine-grained privilege management. With Saviynt, you can secure critical assets such as SAP, Oracle EBS, Office 365, Google Apps, AWS, Azure, Salesforce.com, and Workday.

CyberArk® is a security company that focuses on eliminating the most advanced cyber threats. These threats use insider privileges to attack the heart of the enterprise. CyberArk is dedicated to stopping attacks before they stop business. The company proactively secures against cyber threats before attacks can escalate and do irreparable damage. CyberArk is trusted by leading companies around the world, including 35 of the Fortune 100. The company protects their highest-value information assets, infrastructure, and applications. CyberArk is a global company headquartered in Petach Tikvah, Israel, with U.S. headquarters in Newton, MA. The company also has offices throughout EMEA and Asia-Pacific.

BeyondTrust is a leading provider of Privileged Access Management solutions which offers an integrated and seamless approach to preventing data breaches associated with stolen credentials, misused privileges, and compromised remote access. The platform is designed to easily scale privilege security to address threats that evolve across endpoint, server, cloud, DevOps, and network device environments. With its flexible design, BeyondTrust simplifies integrations, enhances user productivity, and maximizes IT and security investments.

Ping Identity® is a leading independent provider of Identity Defined Security solutions with over 1,400 customers worldwide. It is a trusted solution provider for half of the Fortune 100 companies, as well as industry leaders like Box, Cisco, GE, Kraft Foods, and Walgreens. Through its solutions, Ping Identity secures over one billion identities, enabling hundreds of millions of people to access their favorite applications from any device, anywhere, and transforming the way they live and work daily.
